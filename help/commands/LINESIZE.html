LINESIZE name, size


Description:

Sets the size of any lines drawn on the window or control specified by name. The default size is 1.


Usage:

window "win", "Line Test", normal, 100, 100, 500, 400

Line "win", 10, 10, 10, 100
Line "win", 10, 100, 100, 100
LineSize "win", 5
Line "win", 100, 98, 100, 10
Line "win", 100, 10, 12, 10
