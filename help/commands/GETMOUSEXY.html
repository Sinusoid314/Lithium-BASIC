GETMOUSEXY xVar, yVar


Description:

Retrieves the position of the mouse cursor and places it in the xVar and yVar variables. The x and y position returned is relative to the window or control that the cursor is currently over.


Usage:

window "win", "Color Blocks", normal, 100, 100, 500, 400
control "draw", "win", "", drawbox, 10, 10, 470, 350

event "draw", "leftbuttondown", BtnDown

pause

sub BtnDown
     var x, y as number
     backcolor "draw", Red
     forecolor "draw", White
     GetMouseXY x, y
     box "draw", x-5, y-5, 10, 10
end sub
