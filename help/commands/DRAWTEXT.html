DRAWTEXT name, text, x, y


Description:

Draws a string of text specified by text on the window or control specified by name at the position specified by x and y.


Usage:

window "win", "Graphics Test", normal, 100, 100, 600, 400
control "draw1", "win", "", drawbox, 100, 50, 300, 300

DrawText "draw1", "ALL YOUR BASE ARE BELONG TO US", 20, 100

pause
