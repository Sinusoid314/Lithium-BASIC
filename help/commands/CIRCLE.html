CIRCLE name, x, y, radius


Description:

Draws a circle onto a window or control specified by name, using its current background and foreground colors. The x and y specify the position the circle should be drawn at, and radius specifies the length between the center of the circle to any point on its edge.


Usage:

window "win", "Graphics Test", normal, 100, 100, 600, 400
control "draw1", "win", "", drawbox, 100, 50, 300, 300

circle "draw1", 30, 30, 100

pause
